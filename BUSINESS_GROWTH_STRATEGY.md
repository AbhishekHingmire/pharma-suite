### Pain Points (Current Industry Challenges)

#### Inventory Management Issues
- ‚ùå **Manual batch tracking**: High risk of expired stock sales (regulatory penalty)
- ‚ùå **Stock visibility**: No real-time inventory across locations
- ‚ùå **Expiry losses**: 3-5% of inventory wasted due to expiry
- ‚ùå **Scheme tracking**: Manual calculation of manufacturer schemes leads to revenue leakage

#### Sales & Distribution Challenges
- ‚ùå **Credit management**: 20-30% of sales on credit with poor tracking
- ‚ùå **Bad debts**: 2-4% of sales written off due to non-payment
- ‚ùå **Order processing**: Manual, error-prone, time-consuming
- ‚ùå **GST compliance**: Complex, requires dedicated staff

#### Workforce Management Issues
- ‚ùå **Field force tracking**: No visibility of medical reps/delivery staff
- ‚ùå **Attendance**: Manual registers, proxy attendance common
- ‚ùå **Performance tracking**: Sales targets not linked to actual performance
- ‚ùå **Territory management**: Overlapping territories, inefficient route planning

#### Financial Issues
- ‚ùå **Payment collection**: Delayed collections, no systematic follow-ups
- ‚ùå **Working capital**: Blocked in inventory and receivables
- ‚ùå **Reconciliation**: Time-consuming bank/supplier reconciliation
